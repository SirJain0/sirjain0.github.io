Shredded Letters

It is never easy to verbalize your feelings. Oftentimes we withhold them: either out of fear of judgement, or fear of oversharing. Or, perhaps, out of the fear of tarnishing an otherwise perfectly curated image of yourself.

Songwriting has always been a passion of mine, but these past couple of years have accelerated that into something bigger - a floatation device when I needed it the most. It felt like the most organic way to process my emotions and attempt to contextualize my multidimensional feelings.

This project embodies the concept of buried emotions, where every track represents a withheld feeling, an unspoken confession… in other words, a metaphorical, torn-up letter after a spillage of ink.

I had the absolute best time writing and recording this record over the past year and a half. It was very much something I needed to do for myself, to feel a sense of closure… and perhaps to open doors for new beginnings.
