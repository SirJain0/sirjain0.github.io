Compoundly

Role: UI/UX Designer

It's been said that compounding is the most powerful force in nature. Now, bring that force in the palm of your hand. Calculate compounded annual growth rates in seconds.

Ever wondered what's the growth rate for a profitable trade or an investment decision? Now, you don't need to create a fancy spreadsheet, look up formulas or dive into mental math! Compoundly provides an easy way to quickly estimate the compounded annual growth rates (CAGR) from a starting value to an ending value. It allows for easy selection of number of counting years based on intuitive UX components.

The result shows the compounded growth rate and yearly projected values based on user's input values. There's a beautiful visual of the growth graph. It also provides the ability to track each yearly value along the growth curve using touch gestures. Compoundly helps bring rationalism into decision making.
